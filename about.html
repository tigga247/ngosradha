Children with intellectual and developmental disabilities (IDD) are individuals who have difficulties with cognitive and intellectual functioning. These difficulties can impact their daily activities, including learning, communication, and personal care. IDD can be present from birth or may develop later in life as a result of an injury or illness.
Some common difficulties associated with IDD

1...Difficulty with language and communication: Children with IDD may have difficulty with speaking, understanding spoken language, and using gestures or signs to communicate
2...Delays in motor development: Children with IDD may have difficulty with gross motor skills (such as walking and running) or fine motor skills (such as writing or using utensils).
3...Learning disabilities: Children with IDD may have difficulties with learning and retaining new information, and may require specialized educational support.
4...Social and emotional challenges: Children with IDD may struggle with socializing and building relationships, and may have behavioral and emotional challenges.
5...Adaptive skills: Children with IDD may have difficulty with everyday tasks such as self-care, dressing, and personal hygiene.
It's crucial to remember that every kid with IDD has different issues, and both the degree and nature of those challenges can vary substantially. Many kids with IDD may live happy, meaningful lives with the correct care and treatment.

ABOUT Sharadha

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to providing support and services to individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities (IDD) and their families. Our team is comprised of experienced professionals, including therapists, educators, and social workers, who are committed to helping those with IDD reach their full potential.

Our center offers a wide range of programs and services, including educational and therapeutic programs, rehabilitation services, and support for caregivers. We also provide resources and support for individuals with IDD to help them lead independent and fulfilling lives.

We believe in the inherent value and dignity of every person, and we strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment for all. If you or a loved one is living with IDD, we encourage you to contact us to learn more about the support and services we offer. Together, we can work towards a brighter future for those with IDD.

Thank you for considering our center. We look forward to working with you and your family."

OUR offerings

Support and advocacy

This includes providing legal and financial assistance to families of M.C and C.P children, as well as lobbying for their rights and better care services.

Health and medical care

Ensuring that M.C and C.P children have access to proper medical care and treatment, including regular check-ups and specialized therapy services.

Social support and inclusion

Providing social support and inclusion opportunities for M.C and C.P children, such as recreational activities and community events, to help them feel included and valued.

Housing and transportation

Assisting with the provision of suitable housing and transportation for M.C and C.P children, to ensure they have a safe and accessible living environment.

Counseling and therapy

Providing counseling and therapy services to M.C and C.P children and their families, to help them cope with the challenges they face and improve their mental health and wellbeing.

Funding and resources

Raising funds and securing resources to support the welfare of M.C and C.P children, including donations, grants, and partnerships with other organizations.
